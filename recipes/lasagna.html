Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a step by step guide on how to make lasagna!

Enjoy the dish!

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Seasoning
  • Pastasheets
  • Minced meat

Steps

  1. Cook the minced meat and season it.
  2. Get a glass tray and put the pastasheets at the bottom.
  3. Layer the sheets with tomatosauce and the meat u cooked.
  4. make different levels with the same combo.
  5. Top it off with cheese.
  6. Cook in over at 200°c and enjoy!
