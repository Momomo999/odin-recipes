Muffin Recipe

Description

This is a step by step guide on how to make muffins!

Enjoy the dish!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Put the dough and the yeast together with the eggs.
  2. Mix up till its one texture.
  3. Put the mixture in a muffincup.
  4. Add sugary to taste
  5. Top it off with sprinkels by choice.
  6. Bake in over at 200°c and enjoy!
