Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a step by step guide on how to make salad!

Enjoy the dish!

Ingredients

  • Cucumber/li>
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Black seeds

Steps

  1. Cut the cucumber in thin slices.
  2. Sprinkle some vinegar over the cut pieces in a bowl.
  3. Get black seeds all over the cucumbers.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Have fun!
